Chocolate Cake Recipe

Ingredients:
- 1 and 3/4 cups (220g) all-purpose flour
- 2 cups (400g) granulated sugar
- 3/4 cup (75g) unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 large eggs
- 1 cup (240ml) whole milk
- 1/2 cup (120ml) vegetable oil
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1 cup (240ml) boiling water

Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
2. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla to the flour mixture and beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
4. Stir in the boiling water (the batter will be thin). Pour batter evenly into the prepared pans.
5. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
6. Cool for 10 minutes; remove from pans to wire racks. Cool completely before frosting.
7. Frost with your favorite chocolate frosting and enjoy!
